## Style Prompt

Create a nostalgic indie pop track with vintage warmth and modern production. Think Clairo meets Taylor Swift's "folklore" - intimate, bedroom pop aesthetic with polished songwriting. Use analog-sounding synths, lo-fi drum samples, gentle acoustic guitar, and close-mic'd vocals. The production should feel like flipping through an old photo album - warm, faded, precious. Include vinyl crackle elements and tape saturation for vintage texture. Build from whisper-quiet verses to full-band choruses with emotional payoff.
