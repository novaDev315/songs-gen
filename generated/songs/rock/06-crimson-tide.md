## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Recording Approach**:
- Double-track rhythm guitars for thickness
- Single lead guitar through entire chain (overdrive + distortion)
- Room mic on drums for natural ambience
- Vocal compression to maintain aggression control

**Mix Balance**:
- Guitars: 40% (panned L/R for rhythms)
- Vocals: 30% (center, prominent)
- Drums: 20% (kick and snare center, toms/cymbals spread)
- Bass: 10% (center, felt more than heard)

**Tone Settings**:

**Rhythm Guitar**:
- Gain: 7/10
- Bass: 5/10
- Mids: 3/10 (scooped)
- Treble: 7/10
- Presence: 6/10

**Lead Guitar**:
- Gain: 8/10
- Bass: 6/10
- Mids: 6/10
- Treble: 7/10
- Delay: 300ms, 30% mix
- Reverb: Hall, 20% mix

**Bass**:
- Drive: 5/10
- Tone: Aggressive pick attack
- Compression: Heavy for consistency

## PERFORMANCE NOTES

**Vocal Delivery**:
- Verses: Controlled anger, building intensity
- Pre-Chorus: Opening up, preparing for explosion
- Chorus: Full power, anthemic delivery
- Bridge: Reflective but intense
- Final Chorus: Maximum energy, everything out

**Guitar Performance**:
- Maintain tight palm muting in verses
- Let power chords ring in chorus for maximum impact
- Solo should tell a story: start melodic, build to chaos, resolve

**Drum Performance**:
- Tight pocket in verses
- Explosive fills into chorus
- Double bass in chorus for driving energy
- Creative fills in transitions

**Bass Performance**:
- Lock with kick drum
- Aggressive pick attack for definition
- Occasional octave jumps for dynamics

## LYRICAL THEMES

**Primary Theme**: Transformation through anger - the crimson tide represents both destructive rage and cleansing transformation (PHOENIX)

**Secondary Theme**: Pack strength and primal energy - the collective power of standing together (WOLF)

**Color Symbolism**:
- Red/Crimson: Anger, blood, passion, transformation
- Fire: Purification, destruction, rebirth

**Emotional Arc**:
1. Verse 1: Recognition of inner rage
2. Pre-Chorus: Acceptance of transformation
3. Chorus: Embracing the power
4. Verse 2: Finding strength in pack
5. Bridge: Reflection on the journey
6. Final Chorus: Complete transformation

## LIVE PERFORMANCE NOTES

**Stage Setup**:
